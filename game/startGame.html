How to Play
Press SPACE to start the game;

Move the paddle with your arrows or the mouse;
← Left arrow - moves the paddle left;
→ Right arrow - moves the paddle right;

There is a total of 5 levels;
On each level, another row of bricks is added;

The ball bounces off the walls and the paddle;
If the ball misses the paddle and hits the bottom wall, a life is lost;
Inital amout of lives is 3;
If you lose all 3 lives, you lose the game;
Press SPACE to start a new game or go back to menu
through browser navigation;

Good luck and have fun!!
